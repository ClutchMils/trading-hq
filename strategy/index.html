Trading Strategy

Rules, not opinions

Core Principle

The strategy is built on structure, not prediction.

Every trade must align with higher timeframe direction and key levels.

Entry Rules

  • Trade in direction of higher timeframe bias
  • Price must be at a key level
  • Market structure must be clean
  • Entry must align with structure
If conditions are not clear, there is no trade.

Risk Rules

Risk is fixed. Always.
  • Risk per trade: 1%
  • No increasing risk after losses
  • No revenge trading
If risk is not controlled, nothing else matters.

Exit Rules

  • Stop loss placed at structural invalidation
  • Let trade play out based on structure
  • Do not close early out of fear
The market decides the outcome. You manage risk.

Final Rule

If you break your rules, the result does not matter.

Consistency comes from discipline, not outcomes.

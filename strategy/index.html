My Trading Strategy

This strategy exists to keep me disciplined. If I break these rules, I accept the consequences.

Pre-Trade Checklist

Market I Trade

Forex (major pairs)

Timeframes

  • Bias: Daily
  • Levels: 4H
  • Entries: 1H

Market Conditions

  • Trending markets only
  • Clear structure
  • No high-impact news

Setup Criteria

  • Price at HTF level
  • Bias aligned with structure
  • Clear rejection or continuation

Entry Rules

  • Entry on 1H confirmation
  • No impulse entries

Risk Management

  • 1% risk per trade
  • Hard stop loss
  • Minimum 1:2 R:R

Exit Rules

  • Partial at 1R
  • Trail or hold based on structure

When I Do NOT Trade

  • Choppy ranges
  • Emotional state
  • After 2 consecutive losses

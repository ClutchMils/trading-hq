What Are We Actually Trading?

You are not trading indicators.
You are not trading patterns.
You are not trading lines on a chart.

You are trading people making decisions with money.

Every candle exists because someone bought and someone sold.
That's it. No magic.

Price moves because orders hit the market.

Why Price Moves (The Simple Truth)

Price only moves for one reason:

| More aggressive buyers than sellers, or vice versa.

If buyers are more aggressive, price goes up.
If sellers are more aggressive, price goes down.

News doesn't move price by itself.
Indicators don't move price.
Price moves when money is committed.

Everything else is noise.

Who Is In the Market?

  • Retail traders
    • Small accounts. Emotional. Most Lose
  • Institutions & funds
    • Large money.They move price.
  • Banks & Liquidity providers
    • Provide liquidity. Control Flow

Price respects levels because big money has business there.

Why most beginners lose

Because they:

  • Chase entries
  • Ignore risk
  • Trade every move
  • Thinks the market "owes" them

The market does not care about your account.
It does not know you exist.

If you don't respect that, it will take your money.

What this means for you as a trader

Your job is not to predict.
Your job is to react.

You wait.
You observe.
You protect capital.

Survive first.
Profit comes later.

If you understand this, you're already ahead.

What Support & Resistance Actually Is

Support and resistance are not magic lines

They are areas where:

  • buyers previously stepped in
  • sellers previously stepped in

Price reacted there before, so it might react again That's it.

The Biggest Lie Beginners Believe

Beginners think:

| "If price touches the line, it must reverse."

Wrong.

Price does not respect your line.
Price respects orders

Your line are just guesses of where orders might be.

Why Zones Matter More Than Lines

Markets don't turn on a single price.
They turn in areas

That's why:

  • thick zones > thin lines
  • ranges > exact levels

If your level has to be "perfect," it's fragile

How to Draw Levels That Actually Matter

Start high. Always

  • 1. Go to Daily
  • 2. Mark:
    • obvious highs
    • obvios lows
  • 3. Drop to 4H
  • Refine zones, don't add clutter

If you can't see the level in 5 seconds, it's not important

Why Price Breaks Levels

Levels breaks because:

  • orders get filled
  • imbalance remains
  • new information enters

A broken level is not "fake."

it's information.

What Support & Resistance is Used For

Support & Resistance used to:

  • define risk
  • frame trades
  • avoid bad entries

Not to:

  • predict reversals
  • force trades
  • feel smart

The Truth Most Tradesrs Avoid

Levels don't make you money.

Discipline does.

If you overtrade levels, you'll bleed slowly.

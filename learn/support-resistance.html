Lesson 3: Support & Resistance

The right way

What It Actually Is

Support & resistance are not magic lines.

They are areas where:

  • Buyers previously stepped in
  • Sellers previously stepped in

Price reacted there before, so it might react again.

The Biggest Lie

“If price touches the line, it must reverse.”
Wrong.

Price does not respect your line. Price respects orders.

Your lines are guesses of where orders might be.

Why Zones Beat Lines

Markets don’t turn on a single price.

They turn in areas.

Better

  • Thick zones
  • Ranges

Weak

  • Thin lines
  • Exact levels
If your level has to be perfect, it’s fragile.

How to Draw Levels That Matter

Start high. Always.
  • Go to Daily
  • Mark obvious highs & lows
  • Drop to 4H
  • Refine zones (don’t clutter)
If you can’t see the level in 5 seconds, it’s not important.

Why Levels Break

  • Orders get filled
  • Imbalance remains
  • New information enters

A broken level is not fake. It’s information.

What It’s Actually For

Use it to

  • Define risk
  • Frame trades
  • Avoid bad entries

Not to

  • Predict reversals
  • Force trades
  • Feel smart

The Truth Most Traders Avoid

Levels don’t make you money.

Discipline does.
If you overtrade levels, you’ll bleed slowly.
